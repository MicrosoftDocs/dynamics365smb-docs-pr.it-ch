<span data-ttu-id="7151f-104">Dopo avere creato i solleciti di consegna e apportato le modifiche necessarie, è possibile stampare i report di test o emettere i solleciti di consegna.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7151f-104">After you have created delivery reminders and made any needed modifications, you can either print the test reports or issue the delivery reminders.</span></span>

## <a name="to-print-test-reports-before-issuing-delivery-reminders"></a><span data-ttu-id="7151f-106">Per stampare report di test prima di emettere solleciti di consegna</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7151f-106">To print test reports before issuing delivery reminders</span></span>  

1.  <span data-ttu-id="7151f-107">Scegliere l'icona ![Cerca pagina o report](../../media/ui-search/search_small.png "icona Cerca pagina o report"), immettere **Sollecito di consegna**, quindi scegliere il collegamento correlato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7151f-107">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](../../media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **Delivery Reminder**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>
